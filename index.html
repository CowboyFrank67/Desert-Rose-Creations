Hello.

I am what most people call a busy bee. I can't sit still for long. I have a creative vibe that just won't shut down. I dabble in a variety of materials that include wood, cloth and epoxy. Most, if not all, of my creations start as an idea that gets stuck in my mind and then I can't stop until it transforms into a finished product.

Some of my creations include raised planter boxes:

These beauties are made out of treated 4x4's for the legs and cedar planks for the body and bottom. I made two different sizes. 1. 30"Wx60"Lx30"D and 2. 30"Wx120"Lx30"D.

Seasonal yard ornaments:

These fun little guys are about 30" tall and 12" wide. They can be made with one side or two (Scarecrow on one side and Snowman on the other). Or with any other holiday motif.

Decorative candy jars:

These little guys have a base, a bowl, and a lid. They can be made for any season, or with any motif. They are the cutest little guys in my collection.

Decorative tumblers:

These tumblers are always one of a kind. Each tumbler will turn out different than the last. Great for personalizing your gift to anyone you know!

My newest venture has led me to T-Shirts:

Everyone could use a good t-shirt. I see or hear things and BOOM a new idea starts brewing. There is almost nothing that can't be made into a T-Shirt, copyrighted materials excluded. My favorite right now is the boot T-shirt, "Stuck between Yee Haw and Hell Naw". Don't we all have days like that?
